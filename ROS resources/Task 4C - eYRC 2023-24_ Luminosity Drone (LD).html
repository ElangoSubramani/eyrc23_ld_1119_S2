Task 4C

Prepare of LED Clusters

Aim:

To prepare LED clusters for future tasks.

Requirements

  • Perfboards
  • Infrared LED's
  • 10 ohm resistors
  • AAA Cell and cell holder
  • Switches
  • Wires
  • Soldering kit

Procedure:

  • You can access the circuit diagram for LED clusters by following link.

  • Ensure you follow the provided pictures and circuit for reference .

Points to be remembered: The perfboards supplied have a parallel connection, simplifying the soldering process. Use the larger LEDs for clusters of 2 and 3, and the smaller ones for clusters of 4 and 5. Double-check your connections before connecting the battery to prevent LED damage.

  • After assembling the clusters, confirm the functionality using your mobile camera or the onboard drone camera. Run server.py as done in task3C to validate the circuit's operation. Make sure that blue filtered is installed on the drone.

Submission Instructions

  • Step 1: You need to take two pictures one from the drone camera and another from the mobile camera, make sure that all the clusters are visible in single frame.

  • Step 2: Rename the files to LD_<team_id>ledcluster_drone.jpeg and LD_<team_id>ledcluster_mobile.jpeg. Zip both the files and name the zip file as LD<team_id>task_4c.zip. Submit the .zip file on the portal in place of task 4C.

    This should be the structure of zip file
    |__LD_1234_task_4c.zip
    … |____LD_1234_ledcluster_drone.jpeg
    … |__LD_1234_ledcluster_mobile.jpeg

Deadline

The deadline for Task 4A is 4th January 23:59
